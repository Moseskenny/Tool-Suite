ToolSuite Privacy Policy
Effective Date: March 29, 2026

At ToolSuite, we believe your data belongs to you. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information in our desktop application.

1. Zero Data Collection
ToolSuite is a local-first application.

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

We do not use tracking cookies, analytics, or third-party telemetry.

Your files (images, documents, and spreadsheets) never leave your computer.

2. Local Processing
All core features — including the Image Compressor, File Organizer, Doc Converter, and Expense Analyzer — execute entirely on your local hardware using our built-in Python engine. No data is ever uploaded to a cloud server for processing.

3. Internet Access
ToolSuite does not require an internet connection to function. Internet access is only used if you manually click on external links (such as the Support page or GitHub repository) which will open in your default web browser.

4. Third-Party Services
The software is self-contained. We do not share any information with third-party services because we do not have access to your information in the first place.

5. Contact
If you have any questions about this "No-Data" policy, you can reach out via GitHub: https://github.com/Moseskenny
